What it Is?

CarSell is a system used for Inventory Management, Client & Sales Management, HR Management.

Responsiveness:

This system has a responsive layout in Mobile, Tablet, Laptop, Monitor.

General Features:

  • Functioning Searchbar

  • Dropdown Filters

  • Sort Bottons

Dashboard:

This is page contains total cars in inventory, total employee currently in work, total active clients, total sales, sales trend chart that shows the current and the previous 4 months total sales, then we have a part that shows the stacks that are low, table that shows new clients, most bought cars, and pending tasks that you can add and remove a pending task.

Inventory

Here, all the cars that have touched the entrance of the Dealership is logged. You can add New Cars in the dealership in the inventory and add New Stocks to an existing Car in the log.

Client:

Just like in inventory you can add New Client to the log, while adding, you are to set all the important infromation of the client and also the car that He/She/They Have purchased. if the exixting client buys New Car, it is possible to add it to the clients information and it will automatically update everything in the page that this function will affect. Changing the Status and Privilages of the client is also possible.

Sales:

Nothing much, it's just when a sale is made, it will automatically be logged individually here.

Employee:

This page is the most packed feature page. just like in client you can and employee to the log while setting all the important information of the employee. If employee information needs update, it is possible to edit their information. an emergency clock-in & out.
